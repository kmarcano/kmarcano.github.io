Meet the CEO

Julissa Puello was born and raised in the Dominican Republic. She is very passionate about all her projects and has a great heart that leads her to help the community. She founded Luatani's Corner with the desire revive her homecountry holiday traditions.

Mission

Luatani's Corner aims to bring joy to its clients with every bottle.

Vision

Luatani's Corner aims to create a loving and caring community by creating long-lasting bonds with the community.
